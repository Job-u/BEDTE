BEDTE: Bridging Endangered Dialect To English or Tagalog

BEDTE is a system designed to address the decline of rare languages like Casiguran Agta by leveraging artificial intelligence and speech recognition technology.

about us

Welcome to BEDTE!

We are a passionate team of innovators committed to using cutting-edge technology to preserve and revitalize endangered languages in the Philippines. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional linguistic heritage and modern technology by developing a speech recognition system that accurately translates and documents rare languages like Casiguran Agta.

    Speech-to-Text Translation Services

    Our system converts spoken words in endangered languages like Casiguran Agta and Alibata into accurate text, providing translations into Tagalog and English. This service is invaluable for linguists, educators, and researchers working on language preservation.

    Interactive Language Learning Tools

    We offer user-friendly tools for learning endangered languages, including pronunciation guides, vocabulary building exercises, and real-time feedback on speech accuracy. These tools are designed to make language learning engaging and accessible for all users.

    Linguistic Data Collection and Analysis

    Collaborating with native speakers, we gather high-quality linguistic data and analyze it to improve AI models. This ensures the preservation of not only words but also the cultural context and unique characteristics of each language.
